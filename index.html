Why using Kindmetrics?

Here is why Kindmetrics is an excellent alternative to Google Analytics but also other analytics out there.

Simplicity

Kindmetrics is as kind to you as to the visitors. It is simple to set up. The only thing you need to is type two fields and add a code to your website. You are up and running in no-time. The dashboard is simple to use with everything on one page, with details on own pages.

Lightweight script

Kindmetrics script is tiny, around 40 times smaller than Google analytics. It lets your visitors use less bandwidth when visiting your website.

Share your analytics

Share your analytics privately or publically with friends, customers, or your client. It will be the same dashboard as you use yourself, and it will be easily sharable with a unique URL.

No cookies

We save no cookies on your visitor's computers, and we don't collect personal information. So you don't need to ask for consent for saving cookies with our tracking. Read more about our data policy here.

High Privacy focus

Kindmetrics are focusing 200 per cent on privacy. We are following good practice and are obeying users wishes not to be tracked, like Do Not Track (DNT) support. We use less third parties as possible. Those third parties we use should also be privacy-focused and based in EU if possible.

Open-source project

Kindmetrics is open source, so you can check the code yourself and see if it is handling your data correctly. You could also contribute and push the project forward with the things you want or need. Or host the service yourself. Check our github repo here.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use Kindmetrics on multiple sites?

Yes! You can add as many as you like. All the sites pageviews will be counted together against your monthly pageview in your price plan.

How can I start tracking?

You can easily just add an javascript to your html header. Wordpress, Ghost, Squarespace can easily add those in their system. Later documentation will be added for those. Read more how to add your tracking code here.

Will you block me if I reach the limit?

No, we are kind! We won't block or charge for traffic spikes. If you reach over the limit two months in a row we will contact you and you can then decide if you want to upgrade or cancel your account. You will have 2 weeks. No hard feelings at all.

Why isn't Kindmetrics free?

At Kindmetrics we are only selling a product. We won't sell your data or personal information. Our business model is selling an service, not data.

You pay an small fee for still owning your own data. Which we think is fair.
